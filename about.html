About


Although WA has been rank #1 in the country for twice in a row now, it does not account for the unaffordable livability that people here in WA face due to rapid rise in tech industry and drastic cost of real estate. From high taxes to spending majority of income on housing, it's some of the many factors that are driving many people out of WA state.
